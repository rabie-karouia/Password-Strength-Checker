The Impact of Stolen Passwords

Compromised passwords caused 80 percent of all data breaches in 2019, resulting in financial losses for both businesses and consumers.

Impact on Businesses

Internationally, the average cost of a data breach in 2020 for businesses was $3.86 million, according to IBM. However, for the U.S, the average cost was the highest worldwide at $8.64 million. In the manufacturing industry specifically, malware that stole credentials and dumped passwords created 922 cybersecurity incidents in 2020. 73 percent of these incidents were motivated by financial incentives, while with 27 percent of these incidents, the motive was espionage.
